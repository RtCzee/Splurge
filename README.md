## 💡 About Splurge

Splurge is a modern personal budgeting application designed to help users take control of their finances in a simple and intuitive way. The app focuses on smart expense tracking, budget planning, and financial awareness.

The goal is to help users **stay disciplined while still enjoying their money**, making budgeting feel natural instead of restrictive. Splurge is designed for both individuals and couples, supporting shared financial management and collaboration.

---

## 🔮 Vision

Splurge aims to redefine how people interact with their finances by making budgeting more intuitive, collaborative, and realistic for everyday life.

In the future, Splurge will evolve into a smart financial companion that provides personalised insights, predictive spending analysis, and goal-based recommendations.

---

## 🚀 Planned Features

* 💳 Income and expense tracking
* 📊 Visual spending insights
* 🧾 Budget planning tools
* 🔔 Notifications and reminders
* 👥 Shared budgeting (for couples)
* 🎯 Savings goals tracking
* 🧠 Smart spending insights
* 🟢 Splurge allowance system
